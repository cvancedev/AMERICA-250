# America 250 🇺🇸

> As America reaches 250 years, we look back with gratitude, stand today with humility, and pray for generations yet to come.

## Overview

America 250 is a personal project designed to honor the people, principles, and events that shaped the United States over the past 250 years. Through historical milestones, patriotic reflections, scripture, and interactive experiences, the site encourages visitors to remember the past, honor the present, and look forward to the future.

## Features

### 🇺🇸 Hero Section

* Live countdown to July 4, 2026
* Anniversary progress tracker
* Patriotic imagery and messaging

### 📜 History

* America's founding story
* Declaration of Independence excerpt
* Interactive Declaration of Independence modal

### ✝️ Faith & Freedom

* Scripture-based reflections
* Prayer for America modal
* Focus on faith's role throughout American history

### 🪖 Honor & Sacrifice

* Tribute to America's service members
* Memorial reflections
* Prayer for Gold Star Families modal

### 🕰️ Interactive Timeline

* Major events from 1776 through 2026
* Hover interactions with additional historical details
* Historical milestones spanning multiple generations

### 📊 America at 250

* National milestone statistics
* Rotating patriotic and inspirational quotations
* Reflections on America's journey

### 🔮 Looking Ahead

* Future-focused reflection section
* Biblical encouragement and hope
* Call to preserve faith, freedom, and responsibility for future generations

## Purpose

This project was created as a personal tribute to America's 250th Anniversary. It is intended to inspire reflection, gratitude, remembrance, and hope while honoring the nation's history and those who sacrificed to preserve its freedoms.
